A new window should open, showing the NLTK Downloader. 
Click on the File menu and select Change Download Directory. 
For central installation, set this to C:\nltk_data (Windows), /usr/local/share/nltk_data (Mac), 
or /usr/share/nltk_data (Unix). Next, select the packages or collections you want to download.
